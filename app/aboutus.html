About us

We are a team of four Health Informatics B.Sc. students from Technische Hochschule Deggendorf (THD).

Our vision is to help autistic children feel more comfortable and less stressed in a world that is designed for the neurotypical mind.

We aim to achieve this by helping parents and caregivers of autistic children in Rottal-Inn, find social entertainment venues, suited to their child’s specific needs and requirements.

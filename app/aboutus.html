About us

We are Ali Badran, Franziscka Wojtkowski, Paya Karimi, and Paniz Ziaie, 4th semester health informatics students leveraging our expertise in healthcare, technology, business management, and user experience design to improve the quality of life for individuals with autism and their families.

Our mission is to revolutionize the entertainment landscape for autistic children by providing a comprehensive platform that simplifies the search for suitable venues through advanced filtering, real-time updates, and community insights, promoting accessibility and enriching experiences.

This project is vital as it addresses the significant challenges faced by autistic children and their families in finding appropriate venues, fostering an inclusive society where every child can participate in everyday activities without barriers. We offer a platform with tailored sensory filters, real-time updates, community reviews, and custom venue requests, ensuring a comfortable, enjoyable, and inclusive environment for all.
